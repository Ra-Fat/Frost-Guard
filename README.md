# Frostguard: The Winter Siege

## Overview
**Frostguard: The Winter Siege** is a tower defense strategy game where players defend their castle from waves of enemies in a magical, endless winter world. Players place and upgrade towers, manage resources, and plan strategically to survive increasingly difficult enemy waves.

## Game Overview
- **Title:** Frostguard: The Winter Siege  
- **Genre:** Tower Defense / Strategy  
- **Target Audience:** Players aged 10 to 35 who enjoy strategic planning and resource management.  
- **Goal:** Defend the Great Hearth at the castle core from waves of enemies by placing and upgrading defensive towers.

---

## Story & Setting
The Kingdom of Aethelgard has fallen into an endless magical winter. Your castle, Frostguard, is the last bastion against the Void Horde — a relentless army of cold-based monsters. As King, you must protect the Great Hearth and prevent the destruction of your kingdom.

## Core Gameplay

### Player Abilities & Interactions
- **Place Towers:** Build Archer, Mage, and Paladin towers on designated high-ground platforms.  
- **Upgrade Towers:** Improve tower damage, range, speed, and unlock elemental abilities.  
- **Manage Resources:** Earn Gold by defeating enemies; spend Gold on building or upgrading towers.  
- **Strategic Placement:** Towers can only be placed on specific spots. Placement strategy is crucial.  
- **Wave Preparation:** Reposition or sell towers before each wave starts.

### Enemy Behavior
- Enemies spawn from multiple routes and march towards the castle core.  
- Types include:  
  - **EnemyBone:** Tough skeleton-like enemy.  
  - **EnemyRock:** Heavy, slow-moving tank enemy.  
  - **WolfRider:** Fast, agile enemy for rushing defenses.  
- Enemies deal damage to the castle core if they reach it.

### Defense Point (Castle Core)
- Represents the player’s health bar.  
- Cannot be upgraded.  
- The game ends if its HP reaches zero.

### Tower Mechanics
- Automatic targeting within range.  
- Damage types: Physical, Magic, Area-of-Effect.  
- Special abilities with cooldowns.

### Resource System
- Gold earned by killing enemies and completing waves.  
- Used for building and upgrading towers.

## Levels & Waves

- **Level 1:**  
  - Total Waves: 5  
  - Enemy Types: EnemyBone, EnemyRock, WolfRider

- **Level 2:**  
  - Total Waves: 9  
  - Enemy Types: EnemyBone, EnemyRock, WolfRider

- **Level 3:**  
  - Total Waves: 15  
  - Enemy Types: EnemyBone, EnemyRock, WolfRider

Waves increase in difficulty with higher enemy counts and stronger enemies as the levels progress.

---

## Gameplay Flow
1. Menu Screen  
2. Strategy Phase (Build, upgrade, sell towers)  
3. Active Defense Phase (Enemies spawn and move)  
4. Transition Phase (Collect rewards)  
5. Repeat until win or lose condition is met.

## How to Play
1. Start the game from the menu.  
2. Select a level (1, 2, or 3).  
3. Place and upgrade towers strategically on available platforms.  
4. Start waves and defend your castle core against incoming enemies.  
5. Manage your Gold and resources carefully to survive.  
6. Survive all waves of the selected level to win the game.
